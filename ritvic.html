About Us

RITVIC, the official ciltural club of NITR, was founded in 2015 and began as a small group performing skits on the streets of our college. The club grew as years passed by and have performed for most of the eminent events in the form of street play, both inside and outside of the college. It
continues to be one of the most active clubs of NITR.

Mission

  • To highlight the social evils in the most vivid way for the people via stage plays, street plays and improvs
  • To reach out to the crowd conveying and potraying the deep aspects of society
  • To promote the culture of acting through street and stage play
  • To build self confidence in club members so that they can express themselves clearly when spealing in public
  • To improve writing skills in Hindi so that we can reach out to the public, effectively and spread awareness
  • To work towards a healthy well being of the club members by performing various physical activities like jogging, stretching and vocal exercises.
